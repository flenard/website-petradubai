Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the buying process work?

The process is straightforward: After identifying your ideal property, we handle all paperwork, negotiations, and legal requirements. You'll sign a Sales Purchase Agreement (SPA), pay a deposit (typically 10-20%), and complete the remaining payments according to the developer's plan. We guide you through each step, whether you're in Dubai or abroad.

What is the Golden Visa?

The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residence visa (5-10 years) available to property investors. If you invest AED 2 million or more in UAE real estate, you qualify for a 10-year renewable visa. This includes your spouse and children, with no sponsor required and no minimum stay requirements.

What are the tax implications?

Dubai offers significant tax advantages: zero income tax on rental income, zero capital gains tax on property sales, and no inheritance tax. This makes Dubai one of the most tax-efficient property investment destinations globally, especially attractive for European investors.

Do I need to be in Dubai to buy?

No, you don't need to be physically present. We regularly assist international clients with remote purchases through Power of Attorney arrangements. You can view properties via video tours, sign documents digitally, and complete the entire transaction without visiting Dubai.

What payment plans are available?

Dubai developers offer flexible payment plans, especially for off-plan properties. Typical structures include 10-20% down payment, 40-60% during construction, and 20-30% on handover. Some developers offer extended post-handover payment plans spanning 3-5 years, making investment more accessible.

How do you select properties?

We carefully curate properties based on developer reputation, location potential, rental yields, capital appreciation history, and build quality. We only recommend projects from established developers with proven track records, ensuring your investment is secure and positioned for growth.
